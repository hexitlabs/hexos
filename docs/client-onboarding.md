# Client Onboarding Guide

## What We Need From You

Setting up your AI assistant takes about **5 minutes** of your time. Here's what we need:

---

### Step 1: Create Your Telegram Bot (1 minute)

1. Open Telegram and search for **@BotFather**
2. Send `/newbot`
3. Choose a name (e.g., "AJ Advert Assistant")
4. Choose a username (e.g., "AJAdvertBot") — must end in "bot"
5. BotFather will give you a token — **send it to us**

> 💡 The token looks something like: `1234567890:ABCdefGHIjklMNOpqrSTUvwxYZ`. Keep it private — treat it like a password.

---

### Step 2: Get Your Telegram User ID (30 seconds)

1. Open Telegram and search for **@userinfobot**
2. Send any message to it
3. It will reply with your ID — **send it to us**

> 💡 Your ID is a number like `123456789`. This ensures only you can talk to your bot.

---

### Step 3: API Key (optional)

If you have your own Anthropic or OpenAI API key, send it to us.
Otherwise, we'll set one up for you (included in your retainer).

---

### That's It! ✨

We handle everything else:
- ✅ Server provisioned and secured
- ✅ AI agents configured and tuned
- ✅ Bot connected and ready
- ✅ You'll receive a link like `t.me/YourBotName` — open it and say hello!

---

## After Setup

### Talking to Your Bot

- Open the Telegram bot link we send you
- Say **"hello"** — your assistant will introduce itself
- Ask it anything about your business, prospects, or pipeline
- It remembers your conversations and gets smarter over time

### What Your Bot Can Do

Your assistant comes with specialized agents that work together:
- 🔍 **Research** prospects and companies
- 📝 **Draft** personalized cold emails
- 📊 **Audit** websites for marketing maturity
- 📋 **Compile** daily briefings and summaries
- 📈 **Track** your outreach pipeline

Just ask in plain language — no special commands needed.

### Adding Team Members

Want your business partner to use the bot too?
1. Have them get their Telegram User ID (Step 2 above)
2. Send it to us
3. We'll add them within the hour

### Support

Message us anytime on Telegram. We monitor your system 24/7.

If something seems off with your bot, just tell us — we can usually fix it in minutes.

A: Yes. Your bot runs on a dedicated server. Nobody else has access to your conversations or data.

**Q: Can I use it on my phone and desktop?**
A: Yes! Telegram works on all devices. Your bot is available wherever Telegram is.

**Q: What if I want to change something?**

**Q: How much does it cost to run?**
